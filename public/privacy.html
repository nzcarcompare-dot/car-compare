Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

NZ Car Compare is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal data and do not sell your personal information to anyone.

Who we are

NZ Car Compare (nzcarcompare.co.nz) is a free vehicle cost comparison tool designed for New Zealand consumers. The site is operated as a sole trader business based in New Zealand.

What information we collect

We collect the following information when you use our site:

  • Usage data via Google Analytics 4 — including your approximate location (country and city), the pages you visit, how long you spend on the site, what device and browser you use, and how you found us (e.g. from a Google search or a shared link). This data is anonymised and aggregated.
  • Vehicle plate numbers — if you enter a NZ registration plate to look up a vehicle, that plate number is sent to the Carjam API to retrieve vehicle details. We do not store plate numbers beyond what is temporarily cached to improve performance.
  • Technical data — standard server logs including IP addresses, which are retained for a short period for security and debugging purposes.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifying information. We do not require you to create an account.

How we use your information

  • To operate and improve the NZ Car Compare service
  • To understand how many people use the site and which features are most useful
  • To identify and fix technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

Cookies

We use cookies through Google Analytics to track usage patterns. These are analytics cookies only — we do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies that follow you across other websites. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third-party services

Our site integrates with and links to the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Carjam — vehicle data lookups. See Carjam's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics — website analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Trade Me Motors — we link to Trade Me search results but do not share your data with Trade Me.
  • Insurance providers — we link to Quashed, AA Insurance, AMI, and Tower quote pages. We do not share your data with these providers.
  • Railway — our hosting provider. Your connection data may pass through their infrastructure.

Data retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months by default. Server logs are retained for up to 30 days. Vehicle lookup cache data is retained for up to 24 hours. We do not maintain any other long-term records of user activity.

Your rights under the NZ Privacy Act 2020

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Ask whether we hold personal information about you
  • Request access to any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate personal information

Given that we collect minimal personally identifiable information, most users will have no personal data held beyond anonymised analytics. To make a privacy request, contact us at the address below.

Children's privacy

NZ Car Compare is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at: privacy@nzcarcompare.co.nz
